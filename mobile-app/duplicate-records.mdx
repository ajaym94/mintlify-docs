---
title: Duplicate Records
description: "You don’t have to re-enter the same record details. Just duplicate the record and modify the details as you want and hit Save."
---

Here’s how to duplicate records and get going:

1. Open your `ZORP App`.
2. Go to the `Table` and click on the `Record` you want to duplicate.
3. Click on the <i class="fa-regular fa-copy"></i> icon.
4. All your fields are pre-populated as per the previous record.
5. Modify as you want. 
<Tip>Please modify all the primary fields to ensure there are no duplicate entires. </Tip>
6. Hit `Create`  button.
